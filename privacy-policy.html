Affir Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Affir handles information when you use the app.

Effective date: April 9, 2026

1. Overview

Affir is designed to work with minimal data. We do not require account creation and we do not sell your personal information.

2. Information We Store

Affir stores app preferences on your device so the app can remember your choices.

  • Theme preference
  • Favorite affirmations
  • Selected affirmation categories
  • Reminder setting (on or off)

This data is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

3. Notifications

If you enable reminders, Affir requests notification permission and schedules local notifications on your device.

Notification content is generated locally from the app's built-in affirmation library.

4. What We Do Not Collect

Affir does not intentionally collect:

  • Account credentials
  • Precise location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos, camera, or microphone content
  • Payment card information

5. Third-Party Services

The app may rely on platform and library services (for example, Apple App Store, Google Play, and device-level notification services) to distribute and run the app. Those services may process technical data under their own privacy policies.

6. Data Retention and Your Choices

Because data is stored locally, you can remove it by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

You can disable reminders at any time in the app settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Affir is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date on this page.

9. Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact us at support@affir.app.
